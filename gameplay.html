gameplay

THE GAME CONSISTS OF THREE MAIN TYPES OF LEVELS: STREET, RIVAL SHOWDOWN, AND TRIALS.

Click the images below to see an example of each level!

STREET

  • Your main objective is to tag every graffiti point that is indicated by arrows. They’re often spots that have already been tagged by rival gangs.
  • Each time you tag a graffiti point, you have to move your joysticks correctly in a certain order to successfully paint the area.
  • You collect spray cans around the map. Once you have enough, you can paint graffiti.
  • You’re graded at the end of each level by points. You can acquire and multiply points by doing tricks, completing your graffiti fast, and having health left at the end of the mission.

RIVAL SHOWDOWN

  • These levels are either trick competitions or races. You must complete a trick an opponent shows you, or race a opponent and beat them, in order to unlock that rival as a playable character.

TRIAL LEVELS

  • These are side missions unlocked after completing the main story.
  • There are three kinds:
    • Jet Graffiti: Spray all the graffiti points in time.
    • Jet Tech: Obtain the top score within a time limit by doing tricks and maintaining speed.
    • Jet Crash: A racing sort of challenge. Reach a designated goal before an opponent and spray graffiti on it to win.
